# How to Edit Your Profile

## Quick Guide

You can edit your profile (A-level subjects, predicted grades, and preferences) at any time after logging in.

## Method 1: Using the Header Menu

1. **Click on your name/avatar** in the top-right corner of the page
2. **Click "Your Profile"** from the dropdown menu
3. This will take you to the profile editing page

## Method 2: From the Dashboard

1. **After logging in**, you'll be on the Dashboard
2. The **Profile Setup** section is always visible and editable
3. You can navigate through the 3 steps:
   - **Step 1**: Select your A-level subjects
   - **Step 2**: Enter your predicted grades for each subject
   - **Step 3**: Set your preferences (region, budget, university size, etc.)

## What You Can Edit

### A-Level Subjects
- Select or deselect subjects you're taking
- Available subjects include: Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, English Literature, History, Geography, Economics, and many more

### Predicted Grades
- For each selected subject, choose your predicted grade:
  - A*, A, B, C, D, E, or U
- You can update grades as your predictions change

### Preferences
- **Preferred Region**: London, South East, South West, Midlands, North West, North East, Scotland, Wales, or Any
- **Maximum Budget**: Annual tuition fee budget (default: £9,250)
- **University Size**: Small, Medium, Large, or Any
- **Course Length**: 3 years, 4 years, 5 years, or Any
- **Career Interests**: Select all that apply (Medicine, Engineering, Business, Law, etc.)

## Steps to Edit

1. **Navigate to Profile** (using Header menu or Dashboard)
2. **Go through the 3-step form**:
   - Use "Previous" and "Next" buttons to navigate between steps
   - Make your changes
3. **Click "Update Profile"** when done (or "Complete Setup" if it's your first time)
4. Your changes will be saved and you'll see a success message

## After Updating

- Your profile is automatically saved to the database
- You can immediately get new recommendations based on your updated profile
- Click "Get Recommendations" to see updated course suggestions

## Tips

- **You can edit anytime**: Even if your profile is "complete", you can always go back and edit
- **Changes take effect immediately**: After saving, new recommendations will reflect your updated profile
- **All fields are optional**: You can update just the subjects, just the grades, or just the preferences - you don't need to change everything

## Current Profile Status

Your profile is considered "complete" when you have:
- ✅ At least one A-level subject selected
- ✅ At least one predicted grade entered
- ✅ Preferences configured

Even when complete, you can still edit any part of your profile!
